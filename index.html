Open Source Work

  • elastic-receipt-token: An ERC20 receipt token that rebases its supply continuously to a supply target while being non-dilutive.
  • TSOwnable-Huff: A Two-Step Transfer Ownable contract written in Huff.

Publications & Talks

  • Ampleforth is Hayek Money: Summarizes the concept of Hayek Money and argues that the AMPL token operated by the Ampleforth protocol is a Hayek Money.
  • c4-common-issues: A collection of common Solidity contract vulnerabilities and gas optimizations.
  • Introduction to solmate: Walks through some solmate contracts, overflows its ERC20 implementation, and explains the assembly in SafeTransferLib.
